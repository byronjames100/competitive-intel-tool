# Competitive Intelligence Summarizer
AI-powered competitive intelligence tool for solo founders and small business owners.
BUS670 Strategic AI for Modern Business — WJU Spring 2026

## What This Tool Does
Paste in text from a competitor's website, press release, or news article.
Get back a structured brief with:
- Company positioning
- Apparent strengths
- Apparent weaknesses
- 3 strategic implications for your business
